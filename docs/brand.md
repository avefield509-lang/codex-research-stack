# Codex Research Stack Brand Kit

## Name

**Codex Research Stack**

Short form: **CRS**

Chinese reference name: **Codex 研究栈**

The public name should stay in English because the repository, plugin, Pages, and visual assets already use `Codex Research Stack`.
The Chinese name is a reader aid, not a replacement brand.

## One-Line Introduction

**Codex Research Stack is a multi-agent research workflow layer for Codex, built to keep serious research projects readable, verifiable, and reusable.**

中文简介：

**Codex Research Stack 是一套面向 Codex 的多智能体研究工作流层，用来让严肃研究项目保持清楚、可核验、可复用。**

## Longer Introduction

Codex Research Stack helps researchers turn Codex conversations into inspectable research projects.
It routes work before tools run, coordinates specialized agents when a task becomes project-shaped, keeps citation and evidence checks visible, and hands verified materials into reusable project files and knowledge tools.

中文说明：

Codex Research Stack 帮助研究者把 Codex 对话整理成可回看的研究项目。它会在工具执行前先解释路线，在任务变成项目时组织专门化智能体协作，让引文、证据、写作和复现检查保持可见，并把可靠材料交接到项目文件和知识工具中。

## Tagline

**Multi-agent research workflows for serious scholarship.**

中文宣传语：

**为严肃学术研究而设计的多智能体研究工作流。**

## Logo Concept

The logo is a **Compass C** inside a hexagonal research frame.

- The **C** stands for Codex, coordination, and citation.
- The **compass** stands for route planning before execution.
- The **hexagon** stands for a research system with durable structure.
- The **teal line** stands for evidence flow.
- The **amber line** stands for review gates and scholarly judgment.

## Voice

Use direct research language:

- Say **route**, **verify**, **handoff**, **review gate**, **project state**, and **reproducible outputs**.
- Avoid vague hype such as “revolutionary,” “fully autonomous,” or “magic.”
- Emphasize visible process, evidence quality, and reusable project memory.
