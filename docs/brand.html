Official Name

Codex Research Stack

Short form: CRS. Chinese reference name: Codex 研究栈.

One-line introduction

Codex Research Stack is a multi-agent research workflow layer for Codex, built to keep serious research projects readable, verifiable, and reusable.

Tagline

Multi-agent research workflows for serious scholarship.

Logo idea

The mark is a Compass C inside a hexagonal research frame.

Compass C

The C stands for Codex, coordination, and citation. The compass points to routing before execution.

Hexagon

The hexagonal frame signals a durable research system rather than a one-off prompt trick.

Two colors

Teal marks evidence flow. Amber marks review gates and scholarly judgment.

Voice

Use direct research language. Avoid vague hype.

Say this

Route, verify, handoff, review gate, project state, reproducible outputs.

Avoid this

Revolutionary, fully autonomous, magic, effortless, or claims that cannot be verified.
