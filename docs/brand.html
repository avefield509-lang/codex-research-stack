VELA

VELA is the portable project lab and automation boundary for Codex. It uses layered sails, evidence traces, navigation nodes, pale blue surfaces, and white space to express calm, reviewable research progress.

The mark combines a lightweight layered wrapper, an evidence path, and navigation checkpoints.

Brand Roles

VELA and HELM are independent brands with one shared product language.

VELA

Versatile Experiment Lab & Automation: portable project structure, handoff templates, validators, and HELM-readable context for Codex.

HELM

Hub for Evidence, Logs & Monitoring: optional local dashboard for project status, evidence, files, logs, checks, and Codex notes.

Visual Principles

Adaptive

Wrap the workflow instead of forcing a fixed app model.

Traceable

Show how materials become evidence and artifacts.

Lightweight

Use quiet surfaces, low visual noise, and clear hierarchy.

Trustworthy

Keep decisions, checkpoints, and handoffs visible.
