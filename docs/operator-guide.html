Operator Guide

Keep state truthful.

VELA is operated by preserving distinctions: materials are not evidence, candidate claims are not findings, and generated suggestions are not project facts until reviewed.

Capture

Save source clues early, including DOI, URL, file path, dataset, platform capture, policy report, field note, or manual note.

Verify

Upgrade materials only when source, access time, verification status, and rights or ethics notes are present.

Handoff

Send Codex bounded tasks with files, constraints, expected output, and known gaps.
