# Evidence Records

Evidence is not the same as raw material.

In VELA, a DOI, URL, file path, dataset reference, platform item, or manual note first enters the project as **material**. It becomes **evidence** only after the researcher records enough provenance information.

## Required Fields

Usable evidence should include:

- source locator;
- access time;
- verification status;
- rights or ethics note;
- linked claim or finding;
- explanation of how the evidence supports that claim.

## Method and Evidence Interaction

Method artifacts do not replace evidence. A codebook, variable dictionary, platform ethics note, or identification strategy can satisfy method requirements, but claims still need evidence bindings and support explanations.

## Why This Matters

Projects become hard to audit when materials and arguments are mixed too early. VELA keeps the distinction visible: unsupported claims can exist, but they remain unsupported until evidence is linked and explained.
