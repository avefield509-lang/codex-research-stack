# Evidence Lifecycle

VELA separates collected material from usable evidence.

## Material

A material is something you collected: DOI, URL, file, dataset, platform capture, note, interview excerpt, policy document, or screenshot.

## Evidence

A material becomes evidence only after you record:

- source locator;
- access time;
- verification status;
- rights or ethics note;
- linked claim;
- support explanation.

## Claim

A claim is a statement you may want to make in a paper, memo, report, or brief. A claim remains a candidate until linked evidence supports it.

## Why The Separation Matters

Research becomes unreliable when notes, evidence, and conclusions are mixed too early. VELA keeps the boundary visible so Codex handoffs and human review stay grounded.
