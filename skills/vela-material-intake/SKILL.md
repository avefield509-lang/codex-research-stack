# VELA Material Intake

Use this skill to place source clues, files, URLs, notes, screenshots, DOI records, datasets, or captures into `materials/`.

Rules:

- Do not mark material as verified evidence.
- Record source identity, collection context, access time when known, and uncertainty.
- If the material may contain private or restricted content, flag it before any public export.
- Suggest the next evidence promotion step rather than silently upgrading the record.
