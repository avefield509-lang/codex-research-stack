# VELA Evidence Promote

Use this skill when a collected material is ready to become an evidence record.

Required fields:

- evidence ID;
- source locator;
- access or capture time when available;
- verification status;
- rights or ethics note;
- linked material path;
- claims it may support or qualify.

Do not invent source locators, dates, DOI values, access status, or verification status.
